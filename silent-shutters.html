This webpage is currently under construction (and until I get out of my rocking chair and decide to do something useful, it’s likely to remain so 😴).

We all die twice: once within the moment of our full and final breath, and once when we’re rembered for a true and final time.

My plan is to eventually turn this page into a seperate project (with it's own GitHub repo); a place of public rememberance, dedication, and reflection.

This page is a continual work-in-progress and is soley indended as a reference for my personal use only. I’ve compiled the following list using numerous online sources — the accuracy of these sources are as yet unvarified.
