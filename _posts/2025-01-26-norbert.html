It’s with utter shock and sorrow that I learned of Norbert’s passing today; that sweet little bundle of cute that brought joy to my darkest of days. Such a sad and irreplaceable loss. I’m completely void of words. 💔

At just three pounds, Norbert proved to millions that size has nothing to do with the size of your heart. Through his years of dedicated service in hospitals, his popular children's books, and his iconic digital high-fives, he brought comfort to those who needed it most and taught us all how to be a little kinder.

Discovered as a tiny puppy and adopted into a life of purpose, Norbert spent over a decade volunteering at Children’s Hospital Los Angeles and various care facilities. With his trademark tongue resting to the side and an endless supply of "high-fours," Norbert possessed a unique ability to comfort and heal the spirits of everyone he met; he also authored multiple award-winning books alongside his human mom, Julie Steines, inspiring children to embrace their own unique gifts.

The world is dimmer without his gentle spirit, but his message will live on forever. Thank you, Norbert, for showing us how to change the world, one small act of kindness at a time. 🐶
