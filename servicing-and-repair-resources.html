Cameras

Securing leather/leatherette coverings and related fixings

  • 3M™ 300LSE (transfer tape) - my first choice; it’s much less messy than glue and can often be easier to remove.
  • Rusco Pliobond® 20/25 (adhesive) - my second choice; for the rare occasions when transfer tape won't cut it (difficult to reach spots, for example, or parts with a limited surface area).
Super Glue, Gorilla Glue, Shoe Glue, etc. should never be used for this task — ignore this warning, and you'll soon discover why!

Lenses

Lubricating helicoids

  • Japan Hobby Tool #10/#30 (helicoid grease)
  • Polar Bear Camera 00/0 (helicoid grease)

Misc

General cleaning of non-sensitive surfaces and materials

  • Fairy Liquid – 0% Perfume & Dyes (dish soap)

Removing degraded rubber coatings (a.k.a. “geriatric jelly”) from plastics

  • Sabre Antibacterial Hand Sanitiser (70% alcohol gel)

Removing sticky labels and rust-proofing tools

  • WD-40®

Removing scratches from/polishing acrylic

  • PlexiRevive®
